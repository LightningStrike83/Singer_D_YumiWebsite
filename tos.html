Art Guidelines

Accepting: OCs, Fanart, Couples, Kemonomimi

Not Accepting: NSFW, Furry/Animal focused, Mecha, Armor-heavy outfits

Note: The default art medium is digital art.
If you wish for your commission to be drawn traditionally, you MUST let me know when ordering. If you want the physical copy, we can work out details.
Due to prices and how fragile paper goods are, I am limiting the physical copies to something I can mail out in a card-sized envelope with a stamp.

Terms of Service

  • -I reserve the right to not accept a commission for any reason.
  • -I can take up to 6 months on the commission depending on my schedule/other commissions. (You can ask for updates after 3 months after it was accepted.)
  • -Commissions are first come, first served. If you need the commission by a certain date/have a deadline, let me know when you order.
  • -Art comms are for your personal use only. Do not post the non-watermark version on your own account. For any other use, please ask me.
  • -Commissioned pieces are only posted to my X/AO3 with permission.

Payment
1. First half of the payment will be made after I accept your commission. (Sketch commissions will pay in full.)
2. Second half of the payment is made after the initial sketch/outline provided and approved. Minor/some revisions can be made at the sketch/outline phase.
All payments are made via kofi in CAD.
